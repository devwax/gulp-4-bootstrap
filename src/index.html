Along the banks of the San Joaquin Delta is River Islands, a place that celebrates the new California.

Classic elements of sky, land and water are the backdrop for this remarkable master-planned community.

Connected to the Tri-Valley, BART and Silicon Valley by ACE Train, you can live here, be everywhere.

The neighborhoods of River Islands have been created to take full advantage of the community's setting and amenities. Some neighborhoods look out to our lakes and others are planned in proximity to our schools, parks and the San Joaquin River.

Education is at the core of our vision for River Islands, with a high-performance K-8 Technology Academy already open on-site, and STEAM campus just across the street. More schools are in our plans, and the region is already home to some of the state's finest universities.
